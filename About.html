Business Background

ClubBraids by Theodore is a vibrant hair braiding business owned by Theodore Moleboheng Senokoane, based in Sharpville, Vaal. Specializing in intricate and creative braiding techniques, ClubBraids caters to individuals looking for professional and personalized hair styling services.

Theodore has become a trusted name in her community, known for delivering beautiful braids that embrace cultural heritage while providing clients with modern, trendy styles.

We offer

  • Box Braids
  • Cornrows
  • Twists
  • Customized Hairstyles

Whether you are a woman, man, or child, ClubBraids has the expertise to meet your braiding needs. Theodore also offers personalized hair care consultations, recommending styles that promote hair health while ensuring your braids are both stylish and long-lasting.

For added convenience, ClubBraids provides mobile services for clients who prefer the comfort of at-home braiding sessions.
